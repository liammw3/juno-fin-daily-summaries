    The business returns question comes up regularly. Fin's current answer is accurate but draws from multiple sources. A single, consolidated "Business Returns at Juno" article covering current capability (Advisor mode), roadmap items (Preparer for 1120/1120-S/1065), and the waitlist link would streamline these conversations.
