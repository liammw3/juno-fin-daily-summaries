Documentation Gaps & KB Improvement Suggestions

3 conversations · HIGH PRIORITY

Reviewer: Why Does It Show Different Results Each Upload?

Multiple users are confused that Reviewer shows different findings across identical uploads. The current KB explains the architecture correctly, but users need a plain-language FAQ answering: "Can Reviewer give me different results even if I didn't change anything?" with a clear "Yes, because..." and guidance on when to ignore vs. investigate discrepancies. Cross-year Gotcha Check comparisons should be explicitly called out as expected behavior.

2 conversations · HIGH PRIORITY

Advisor: How Do I Know When My Upload Is Done?

A dedicated "How to Tell When Your Return Is Ready in Advisor" article would prevent the upload-banner loop. It should include: screenshot of the banner, what "logic executed" means and when it clears, and what to do if the banner never appears (i.e., contact support — it's a bug).

1 conversation · MEDIUM PRIORITY

International Form Support — Update Roadmap Statement

The KB article referenced in conversation 215473635051649 states Advisor will support international law "by the end of 2025." This is past-dated. The article needs an updated timeline or should be revised to say "international support is on the roadmap — contact us for current availability."

2 conversations · MEDIUM PRIORITY

Multi-User & Concurrent Access Explainer

Questions about whether multiple team members can work simultaneously in Juno are recurring. A dedicated "Working With Your Team in Juno" page covering Reviewer (multiplayer), Binder (single-user), Admin vs. Preparer workflows, and the no-per-user-charge policy would reduce these inbound questions.

2 conversations · LOW PRIORITY

Re-Uploading Documents After Export — Feature Discoverability

Users are surprised they can go back and add more documents after an initial export. This capability should be more prominently surfaced in the Preparer guide and possibly in the app itself as a "What's Next?" prompt after export.
